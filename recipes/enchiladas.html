Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Flour Tortilla
  • Corn Tostatas
  • Lettuce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Mexican Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in pan until fully cooked.
  2. Remove the grease from the pan with paper towel.
  3. Pour in 1/3 cup of water.
  4. Mix in taco seasoning with the meat at low heat.
  5. Add all of your ingredients in a flower tortilla.
  6. Cook the tortilla on both sides using a hot pan.
